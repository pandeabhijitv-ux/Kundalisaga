# Divisional Charts (Vargas) in AstroKnowledge

## Overview
Divisional charts are specialized charts that provide deeper insights into specific life areas. While D1 (Rasi chart) gives the overall picture, divisional charts are essential for **accurate predictions** in their respective domains.

## Implemented Divisional Charts

### D2 - Hora Chart (द्विहोरा)
**Purpose:** Wealth, prosperity, material gains
**Use for predictions about:**
- Financial stability and wealth accumulation
- Sources of income
- Material prosperity
- Banking and investments
- Property from wealth perspective

**How to read:**
- Only two signs are used: Cancer (Moon) and Leo (Sun)
- Planets in Cancer indicate wealth through emotions, fluctuating income
- Planets in Leo indicate wealth through authority, stable income
- Benefic planets in D2 indicate good wealth

### D9 - Navamsa Chart (नवांश)
**Purpose:** Marriage, spouse, dharma, inner strength
**Use for predictions about:**
- **Marriage timing and compatibility** (MOST IMPORTANT)
- Spouse characteristics and nature
- Post-marriage life quality
- Spiritual evolution
- Hidden strengths of planets
- Fruits of karma

**How to read:**
- Called "Bhagya Amsa" - fortune chart
- Shows the hidden potential of D1 placements
- Strong D9 can elevate weak D1 planets
- 7th house and 7th lord in D9 for spouse details
- Venus/Jupiter in D9 for marriage timing
- **CRITICAL for marriage predictions**

### D10 - Dasamsa Chart (दशांश)
**Purpose:** Career, profession, reputation, status
**Use for predictions about:**
- **Career success and professional growth**
- Type of profession suited
- **Promotion timing**
- Authority and status in career
- Professional reputation
- Government/private sector success

**How to read:**
- 10th house and 10th lord show career
- Exalted planets indicate professional excellence
- Malefic planets may indicate challenges
- Strong D10 = Strong career even if D1 is weak

### D7 - Saptamsa Chart (सप्तांश)
**Purpose:** Children, progeny
**Use for predictions about:**
- Having children
- Number of children
- Health of children
- Relationship with children
- Adoption matters

**How to read:**
- 5th house and Jupiter for children
- Benefics in 5th house = healthy children
- Malefics may indicate delays or challenges

### D12 - Dwadasamsa Chart (द्वादशांश)
**Purpose:** Parents, especially relationship with them
**Use for predictions about:**
- Parents' health and longevity
- Inheritance from parents
- Relationship quality with parents
- Ancestral property

## How Divisional Charts Improve Accuracy

### For Marriage Predictions:
1. **Check D1** - Basic compatibility, overall picture
2. **Check D9** - MANDATORY for marriage analysis
   - 7th house condition
   - Venus/Jupiter placement
   - Upapada Lagna
3. **Check D7** - For children after marriage
4. **Cross-verify** all three charts

**Example:** A person may have weak Venus in D1 but strong Venus in D9 = Difficult finding partner but excellent married life

### For Career Predictions:
1. **Check D1** - 10th house, 10th lord, career indicators
2. **Check D10** - MANDATORY for career analysis
   - Detailed profession type
   - Success level
   - Promotion timing
3. **Check D2** - For income from career
4. **Cross-verify** timing using Dashas

**Example:** Good D1 career indicators but weak D10 = Job but limited growth. Need to strengthen D10 through remedies.

### For Wealth Predictions:
1. **Check D1** - 2nd and 11th houses
2. **Check D2** - MANDATORY for wealth analysis
3. **Check D10** - Career income
4. **Combine** all three for complete picture

## Using Divisional Charts in Questions

When a user asks:
- **"When will I get married?"** → Analyze D1 + **D9 (primary)** + D7
- **"When will I get promoted?"** → Analyze D1 + **D10 (primary)**
- **"Will I have children?"** → Analyze D1 + D9 + **D7 (primary)**
- **"Will I be wealthy?"** → Analyze D1 + **D2 (primary)** + D10

## Best Practices for Accurate Predictions

1. **Always check the relevant divisional chart**
   - Never predict marriage without checking D9
   - Never predict career without checking D10
   - Never predict wealth without checking D2

2. **Strength of planets in divisional charts**
   - Exalted in divisional chart = Very strong
   - In own sign = Strong
   - Debilitated = Weak, needs remedies

3. **Dasha timing**
   - Check divisional chart during specific dasha periods
   - If Jupiter Dasha + Jupiter strong in D9 = Excellent time for marriage
   - If Saturn Dasha + Saturn strong in D10 = Career growth

4. **Remedies based on divisional charts**
   - Weak Venus in D9? → Venus remedies for marriage
   - Weak Sun in D10? → Sun remedies for career
   - Weak Jupiter in D2? → Jupiter remedies for wealth
