### 2. Universal Spiritual Remedies (Sanskrit + English)

#### A. Ganesh Vandana (Remove Obstacles)
**Sanskrit:**
```
वक्रतुण्ड महाकाय सूर्यकोटि समप्रभ।
निर्विघ्नं कुरु मे देव सर्वकार्येषु सर्वदा॥
```

**English:**
```
Vakratunda Mahakaya Suryakoti Samaprabha
Nirvighnam Kuru Me Deva Sarva-Kaaryeshu Sarvada
```

**Meaning:** O Lord with curved trunk, large body, brilliance of million suns, please make my endeavors obstacle-free always

**Simple Ganesh Mantra:**
- Sanskrit: ॐ गं गणपतये नमः
- English: Om Gam Ganapataye Namaha

**Best For:**
- New beginnings
- Business ventures
- Education & exams
- Interview preparation
- Removing difficulties

#### B. Vishnu Sahasranama (Ultimate Protection)
**Description:** Recitation of 1000 names of Lord Vishnu

**Benefits:**
- Overall protection
- Peace of mind
- Removes all doshas
- Spiritual growth
- Health and wealth
- Mental peace

**Simple Vishnu Mantra:**
- Sanskrit: ॐ नमो नारायणाय
- English: Om Namo Narayanaya

**Timing:** 
- Full recitation: 30-45 minutes
- Partial recitation (100-200 names) also beneficial
- Best days: Thursday, Ekadashi, Dwadashi
- Can listen to recordings

#### C. Other Powerful Mantras (Sanskrit + English):

1. **Gayatri Mantra** - Universal Vedic mantra
   - **Sanskrit:** ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः। तत्सवितुर्वरेण्यं। भर्गो देवस्य धीमहि। धियो यो नः प्रचोदयात्॥
   - **English:** Om Bhur Bhuvah Swaha | Tat Savitur Varenyam | Bhargo Devasya Dhimahi | Dhiyo Yo Nah Prachodayat ||
   - Removes all doshas, enhances all planets
   
2. **Mahamrityunjaya Mantra** - Ultimate healing
   - **Sanskrit:** ॐ त्र्यम्बकं यजामहे सुगन्धिं पुष्टिवर्धनम्। उर्वारुकमिव बन्धनान् मृत्योर्मुक्षीय मामृतात्॥
   - **English:** Om Tryambakam Yajamahe Sugandhim Pushtivardhanam | Urvarukamiva Bandhanan Mrityor Mukshiya Maamritat ||
   - Health and healing, protection from accidents, longevity
   
3. **Durga Saptashati** - 700 verses of Goddess Durga
   - Protection from enemies, overcoming obstacles
   
4. **Hanuman Chalisa** - 40 verses praising Lord Hanuman
   - Removes Saturn afflictions, gives courage and strength
